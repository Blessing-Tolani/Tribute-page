Achievements

Ire Aderinokun is a frontend developer and user interface designer from Lagos, Nigeria. She is the Cofounder, COO, and VP Engineering of BuyCoins (YC S18), a cryptocurrency exchange for Africa. They also built Sendcash, a cryptocurrency-powered remittance platform to easily send money to countries in Africa from around the world. She speaks at conferences worldwide, and organise Frontstack, a conference for frontend engineering in Nigeria. She has written over a hundred technical articles on her blog, bitsofcode. She is one of the founding members of the Feminist Coalition, a group of women with a mission to champion equality for women in Nigerian society. She has a Senior Web Developer certificate in Computer Software Engineering and Blockchain Developer Nanodegree in Computer science from Udacity She is the first Nigerian female to become a Google Developer Expert. she has helped so many women achieve their dream careers by sponsoring them on Udacity nanodegree programs.
